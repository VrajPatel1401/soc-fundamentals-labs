### Objective
To understand how SOC analysts analyze and triage SIEM alerts.

### Alert Description
The SIEM generated an alert for multiple failed login attempts within a short time.

### Analysis Steps
1. Reviewed alert details such as time, source IP, and username.
2. Checked frequency of attempts.
3. Compared with normal login behavior.
4. Determined whether the alert was a true or false positive.

### Outcome
The alert was considered suspicious and required further investigation.

### What I Learned
- Basic SIEM alert triage process.
- Importance of distinguishing false positives.
- How alerts help detect attacks early.
